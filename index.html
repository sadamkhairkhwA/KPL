    Who We Are

    Trusted B2B Pharmaceutical Importer & Distributor in Afghanistan

    Khair Khwa Pharma (خیرخواه فارما) is a Kabul-based licensed B2B pharmaceutical importer and distributor — supplying healthcare institutions and authorized medical partners. We are not a public-facing online pharmacy.

    We import World Medicine (Turkey) products and distribute them through documented logistics to licensed pharmacies, hospitals, clinics, NGOs, and authorized healthcare distributors across Afghanistan. Khair Khwa Pharma does not provide direct-to-consumer pharmaceutical sales.

    Import Partner

    World Medicine

    “Health is a treasure we share together.”

    World Medicine is an international pharmaceutical group headquartered in Turkey, supplying medicines and healthcare products to more than 65 countries — including Afghanistan.

    Khair Khwa Pharma imports through World Medicine to bring GMP-certified products from audited manufacturing sites to pharmacies, hospitals, and clinics across Afghanistan, with full batch documentation and responsible distribution.

    Their corporate mission aligns with our own: contributing to healthier lives through accessible, effective medicines — manufactured under international quality standards and supported by a global supply network.
